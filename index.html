About

My name is Eddie and I’m a graphic designer. I also like to code, and I have a passion for simple interfaces as you can see. Being a creative person I think it`s important to focus on typography, content and conveying the message that you will send out to the world. I also like to take pictures with my digital camera or even my phone. Recording and remembering things that happen around me was my obsession since I was a kid. In case I am not around a computer, most probably you will find me on the beach where I kitesurf.

I’ve been designing professionally for more than a decade, and from early days in my career I was always looking to create wonderful experience that affect people and help them expand their understanding of the world around them, whether I design a logo, corporate identity, create a new product line or a digital experience. So, if you're in need of a skilled partner for amazing brand, print, or web projects, I would love to share some of my work with you.
